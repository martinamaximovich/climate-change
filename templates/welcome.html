How has climate change affected your home?


Climate change is the greatest challenge facing humanity. In particular, rising temperatures pose a number of real dangers: more droughts and more floods, irreversible losses to biodiversity, direct and indirect health threats, higher air pollution, and beyond. To develop mitigation strategies and adapt to the changing planet, we have to understand how we're changing the climate across the globe.

However, why don't we bring this home first? Whether you realize it or not, the ongoing climate changes have been affecting your hometown, too. How has the temperature changed in your hometown?
